  • Your fists are the weakest weapon, but they don't need any ammo
  • While playing, you can only look left and right
  • If an enemy appears above or below you, just shoot at them as if they were at your level and it will automatically aim at the enemy
  • Some boss enemies will appear again as normal enemies in other episodes
  • Holding down the fire button will make your weapon shoot until the button is released
  • You need to hold down the sprint button to keep sprinting
  • You can turn on auto sprint in the pause menu
  • If Doomguy will make a grunting sound if something can not be interacted with
